Meal and Break Periods

Employees who are scheduled to work eight (8) hours or more are entitled to take up to one (1) hour, and not less than thirty (30) minutes, without pay for a lunch break, subject to applicable federal and state laws. Employees are allowed one (1) ten (10) minute paid rest period for each four (4) hours worked for personal needs, such as smoking, restroom stops, or personal phone calls. Your manager will advise you of the meal and break schedule.
