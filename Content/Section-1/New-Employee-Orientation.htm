New Employee Orientation

Orientation is a formal welcoming process that is designed to make the new employee feel comfortable, informed about the company, and prepared for his or her position. New employee orientation is conducted by and includes an overview of the company history and an explanation of the company core values, vision, mission, goals, and objectives. In addition, the new employee will be given an overview of benefits, tax, and legal issues and complete any necessary paperwork.
